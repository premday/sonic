Premday Network is a dedicated stream inside Premday association, for anything related to Networking: SONiC, automation, operations, hardware, design and more.

Our goals are to unite our strengths to:
* Help each other with issues, questions, and tooling.
* Provide vendors with a unified voice from a group of companies.
* Provide vendors with a direct channel to a group of companies.
